Slingshot Rocket


ABOUT THE GAME

STORY

In a far away galaxy once lived some people not to different from us on a totally cool planet.
But after some nuclear wars and a little bit of pollution the planet was not so cool anymore.
And so a brave crew set out in their spaceship to travel through the universe in order to find an even cooler planet.


GAMEPLAY

Navigate the spaceship through a variety of levels by only using the gravity of planets and activating the spaceships thrust in the right moment.
Try to reach the portal as fast as possible whitout damaging your vessel.


FEATURES

This game features:

  • 8 levels with ascending diffuculty
  • A number of different planets which each have a unique effect on the spaceship
  • Original Music
  • 4 different spaceship-designs
